# Customer Segmentation Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer demographics, annual income, and spending behavior to identify meaningful customer segments. The objective is to help businesses understand customer purchasing patterns and make data-driven marketing decisions.

The analysis was performed using Python for data cleaning and exploratory data analysis (EDA), while Power BI was used to create an interactive dashboard for visualization and business insights.

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains the following columns:

| Column Name    | Description                                          |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| customer_id    | Unique identifier for each customer                  |
| age            | Age of the customer                                  |
| gender         | Gender of the customer                               |
| annual_income  | Annual income of the customer                        |
| spending_score | Spending score based on customer purchasing behavior |

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze customer demographics and spending behavior.
* Calculate key business metrics.
* Identify spending patterns across age groups and genders.
* Study the relationship between income and spending score.
* Segment customers into meaningful business categories.
* Generate actionable business recommendations.

## Exploratory Data Analysis

The following analyses were performed:

### Customer Analysis

* Average Age of Customers
* Average Annual Income
* Gender-wise Spending Analysis
* Age Group-wise Spending Analysis
* Income vs Spending Score Relationship

### Customer Segmentation

Customers were classified into four segments:

* High Income – High Spending
* High Income – Low Spending
* Low Income – High Spending
* Low Income – Low Spending

---

## Key Insights

* Customer spending behavior varies significantly across different income groups.
* Higher income does not always lead to higher spending.
* Distinct customer segments exist within the customer base.
* Age and income influence purchasing behavior differently.
* Segmentation enables more targeted marketing strategies.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Target High Income – High Spending Customers

Focus marketing efforts on customers with high purchasing power through premium offerings and personalized campaigns.

### Increase Engagement of Low-Spending Customers

* Loyalty programs
* Discount campaigns
* Product bundles
* Personalized recommendations

### Retain High-Income Customers

* VIP memberships
* Exclusive rewards
* Early access to products
* Premium customer support
